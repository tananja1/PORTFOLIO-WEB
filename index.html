About Us

NOTEL ID is a non-governmental organization based in Ghana west Africa. We train talented, creative, and innovative young people to build on their opotential in the areas of agriculture, Entrepreneurship, and digital Technology.
We provide basic needs to students in the second cycle of education and tertiary education to ensure they have a good living and happy lifestyle.

Our vision is to ensure every passionate person under our mentorship program has a good living standard through creativity and innovation
Poverty eradication is our key focus.

Areas of operation

Agriculture

We aim to train and support passionate yet underprivileged young people, encouraging them to develop an interest inagriculture(agribusiness) as a key source of income and to boost adequate food supplies for local and international consumption. To achieve this, we sponsor every interested person by providing a suitable environment, logistics and startup capital within our scope of operation. These individuals must adhere to our flexible basic rules and regulations.

learn more

Education

We ensure the every passionate young person receives our full support in any field to excel in their education career through creativity and innovation. we provide these young individuals with basic needs to help them manage their educational activities smoothly. However, we encourage them to take these fields seriously aside activities to help them achieve their goals in life.

learn more

Entrepreneurship

We aim to train and empower passionate young individuals to develop small-scale businesses for rapid growth. Young people with a dedicated entrepreneurial mindset are one of the most effective and quickest ways to eradicate poverty and reduce youth unemployment. We fervently hope that all young individuals in our program receive our support to boost their existing small-scale businesses and start new ones as well.

Learn more

Technology

In this modern era, technology directly creates jobs, with the ICT sector being one of the largest employers and expected to remain so. We support talented but underprivileged young people to unearth their true potential through creativity and innovation.
